Alongside my graduate studies, I serve as a **campus missionary** at my university — in the *tentmaking* (자비량) tradition: supporting the mission through my own vocation as a researcher, rather than being supported by it.

I believe a researcher's desk and a missionary's calling are not two separate lives. Doing honest, excellent scholarship is itself a form of witness, and the campus — where students wrestle with questions of truth, meaning, and direction — is my mission field.

#### What this looks like

- Walking alongside students on campus: sharing meals, conversations, and the gospel
- Bible studies and one-to-one discipleship with fellow students
- Pursuing academic excellence as a testimony that faith and scholarship belong together
